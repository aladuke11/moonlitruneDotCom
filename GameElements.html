üîì The Unlock System

Location: LinkTree Clone page

Hidden in the LinkTree Clone project is a "Secret Unlock" button that grants you access to restricted items in the Cyberpunk Store. Click the "Unlock HoloVision Goggles" button to reveal a previously unavailable product.

How it works: The unlock state is saved to your browser's localStorage, meaning once you unlock the goggles, they remain unlocked across sessions.

Game Element: Discovery & Reward - Finding the unlock button feels like discovering a secret level!

üí≥ The Debt System

Location: Cyberpunk Store

When you "purchase" items in the Cyberpunk Store, you're actually taking out a debt with the fictional CryptoCorp. Each purchase adds $500 to your outstanding debt balance.

Features:

  • Debt persists across browser sessions
  • Home page displays your current debt amount
  • Number of purchased items shown on home page reminder
  • Immersive cyberpunk flavor text: "Failure to pay will result in account suspension"

Game Element: Consequence System - Your purchases have narrative weight and consequences!

üì¶ Purchase Tracking

Location: Cyberpunk Store & Home Page

Every item you purchase is meticulously recorded with:

  • Item name
  • Price paid
  • Exact date and time of purchase

You can view your complete purchase history in the browser console (F12) using the function getPurchases().

Game Element: Progression Tracking - Like a quest log, your purchases are recorded for posterity!

üéÆ How to Experience All Game Elements

Recommended progression:

  1. Visit the LinkTree Clone page
  2. Click the "Unlock HoloVision Goggles" button to unlock the secret item
  3. Navigate to the Cyberpunk Store
  4. Notice the goggles are now available (no longer sold out!)
  5. Purchase the HoloVision Goggles to trigger the hidden box
  6. Return to the home page to see your debt warning
  7. Check the browser console (F12) and use getPurchases() to view your purchase history

üîß Developer Notes

localStorage Keys Used:

  • cryptoCorp_debt - Your total outstanding debt
  • cryptoCorp_purchases - Your complete purchase history (JSON array)
  • cryptoCorp_gogglesUnlocked - Whether goggles are unlocked (true/false)
  • goggles_purchased - Whether goggles have been purchased (true/false)

Console Functions Available:

  • getDebt() - Check your current debt
  • getPurchases() - View all purchases
  • clearDebt() - Reset debt (for testing)
  • clearPurchases() - Reset purchase history (for testing)
